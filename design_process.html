Design Motto

The SAP Fiori design guidelines exist to provide a consistent and user-friendly experience across SAP's suite of enterprise software applications. These guidelines serve as a framework for designing intuitive, responsive, and aesthetically pleasing user interfaces. By adhering to Fiori design principles, developers and designers ensure that SAP applications have a unified look and feel, promoting usability and reducing the learning curve for users.

Now, the need for a design system like Fiori extends beyond SAP and applies broadly to any company's product portfolio. A design system acts as a centralized resource that establishes and enforces a set of design standards, components, and patterns. This helps maintain coherency and consistency in the user experience across different products and platforms within a company.

In essence, a design system like SAP Fiori not only benefits the company by streamlining development but also enhances the end-user experience, fostering a sense of familiarity and ease of use across the entire product ecosystem.

Fiori Design Guidelines

The Design Motto for every design project within SAP is based on SAP’s Design Gate principle.

Albeit that it has been tweaked overtime, but the base motto pretty much remains.

Discover – Design – Develop – Deliver

To ensure a smooth glide across all the phases an evaluation process called Design Gates is followed.

It’s a three step process denoted by DG-0, DG-1 and DG-2 (DG - Design Gate).

Each step entails a series of expectations, starting all the way from the purpose of the app.
